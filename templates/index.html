Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book an appointment online?

You can book an appointment by clicking on the “Book Appointments” link in the navigation bar. Select your department, doctor, and time slot to confirm your booking.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, our emergency services are available 24/7. We have dedicated trauma and ambulance services at your disposal.

Can I access my reports online?

Yes, once your tests are complete, you can log in using your credentials to access medical reports under the 'Patient Dashboard'.

Do you have insurance tie-ups?

We have partnerships with most major health insurance providers. Please contact our reception or billing department for more info.

What languages do your staff speak?

Our staff can assist you in English, Hindi, Marathi, and several regional languages for better communication and comfort.
